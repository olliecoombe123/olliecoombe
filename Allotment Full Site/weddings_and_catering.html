weddings + catering

we love to be a part of your special days in whatever way we can - from canapes to dinner to cakes big or small

we currently offer canapes and buffet style catering for larger parties including salads + sides + meat + bread in a bespoke menu created for your event. alongside this we can provide cutlery / crockery hire where necessary.

for smaller events we can also offer a 2 / 3 course menu as long as there is access to a kitchen at your venue. just let us know what you’re looking for + the number of guests you need to feed and we will conjure up some menus for you

need cake? we’ve got you covered. we can make celebration cakes in all sizes and guises, whether you prefer a tower of brownies or a multi-layered floral beauty.

just e-mail us with as much information as you can provide - date / location / flavour ideas / colour scheme / number of guests - and we will work together to create your dream cake. see below for inspiration and feel free to reference these when designing your cake.

we use completely natural decorations, championing home grown flowers + herbs alongisde dehydrated fruits to build up texture + colour
