allotment is a cambridgeshire based micro-bakery + kitchen set-up by founder katie in 2015

here in the allotment kitchen we believe that seasonality is king, creating a great range of products that showcase our amazing local ingredients.

whether you’re after a cake to see you through the weekend, or catering for your party, we can create a bespoke menu tailored to your palette.



we use the finest ingredients, organic wherever possible, to ensure excellent flavour and respect for nature.

the micro-bakery produces a selection of lovingly made sourdough loaves using the excellent flour milled by jon at foster’s mill.

our breads are simply made with flour + water + salt, mixed with care and left to ferment overnight before being baked fresh each morning.

the simplest of pleasures, real bread is at the heart of our operation
